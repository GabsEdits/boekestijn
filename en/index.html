The transport company Boekestijn was established in 1994 in Mill, the Netherlands. Initially, the company operated with a fleet of three trucks, but over time, it has experienced remarkable growth, expanding to over 800 trucks currently in operation. With a rich history spanning over 27 years, we have firmly established ourselves as a prominent player in the international transport and logistics market in Europe. Our extensive presence includes headquarters located in eight countries, supported by a dedicated team of over 300 highly skilled professionals.
